Programs

Swiss precision,
applied to security

Hack CH programmes reflect the Swiss character — rigorously structured, multilingual, and designed to produce practitioners who meet the exacting standards of Switzerland's public and private institutions.

01

Swiss CTF League

A national Capture The Flag competition series across German, French, and Italian-speaking Switzerland — connecting university security clubs in all four linguistic regions.

02

Crypto Research Bridge

A practitioner-to-researcher bridge programme connecting ETH and EPFL cryptography labs with Swiss financial institutions — translating academic breakthroughs into deployable defences.

03

Swiss Red Team Cert

A professional certification in offensive security designed specifically for the Swiss regulatory environment — recognised by FINMA, the Swiss Bankers Association, and federal agencies.

04

International Geneva Lab

A unique programme leveraging Geneva's concentration of international organisations — UN, ICRC, WHO — to develop security professionals for multilateral institutional environments.

05

Kantonspolizei Partnership

Structured collaboration with cantonal police cyber units — developing digital forensics and incident response capabilities for Switzerland's decentralised law enforcement model.

06

Swiss Talent Pipeline

A vetted placement programme connecting Hack CH members with security roles at Swiss private banks, pharmaceutical companies, and federal government agencies.
